Hello! I'm Ashley Doughty and I'd like to share some facts about myself. I grew up across the river from Philadelphia, PA. I moved down to Richmond, VA back in 2021 to be with my now fiancé. He and I love going to new restaurants in the city, but he's more adventurous than I am. I enjoy spending time with friends and also by myself.

I'm a big animal lover and wish for a couple dogs in my future soon. For now, I'll settle on stealing this cutie from my future in-laws. I'm a big gamer in every sense of the word. I'm huge into not only videogames, but tabletops and card games too. I'm very quickly running out of room for my ever growing collection. I'm slowly finding myself being dragged back into Magic: The Gathering by my future husband.

I'm known for my artistic ability. I have a BS in Graphic Design with a minor in art. Many people ask me why I haven't pursued a career in my field. Other than being highly competitive, back when I had graduated, I had major self esteem issues. Still do to this day. I didn't think I could cut it with some of the amazing talent I was surrounded by constantly. As of now, I'm feeling different. I want to head in a direction that can both utilize my background and give me a logical challenge. I had one web design class way back in my curriculum and I always kinda resented that. I had been exploring the idea of coding bootcamps and UX/UI design career shifts. That's when I found Correlation One. I can resume the knowledge years ago and greatly expand on it. Still kind of nervous about the job getting aspect, however, this time around I believe in myself.
